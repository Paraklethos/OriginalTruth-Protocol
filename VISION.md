## 1. The Crisis of Modern AI
Modern Artificial Intelligence is built on **probabilities**, not **principles**. It learns ethics through "social alignment" — a process of mimicking human preferences found in massive datasets. This approach has three fatal flaws:

* **Semantic Drift:** Words like "Justice" or "Freedom" change meaning based on political fashion, leaving the AI without a stable moral compass.
* **The Tyranny of the Average:** By following the "mean" of the data, AI suppresses the vital dissonance of the minority, which is often where the truth resides.
* **The Black Box of Moral Debt:** A probabilistic model cannot be "responsible" because it doesn't "understand" the weight ($\mathbf{D}$) of its actions; it only predicts the next most likely token.
